ABOUT ME

Coluatura Soprano

My name is Tess Cunningham and I’m currently a student at Loyola University New Orleans studying Music Industry and minoring in classical voice. I've been taking vocal lessons and participating in choirs for 8 years. I now take private vocal lessons with Dreux Montegut and have been in choirs conducted by Dr. Meg Frazier. I’m skillful in sight singing and can learn classical pieces quickly. My repertoire includes pieces in French, Italian, Latin, English and German.
